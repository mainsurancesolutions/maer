Data Handling Policy

Last updated: July 2025

What we collect

ContractsCompare is designed to minimize data collection. We do not require accounts, logins, or personal information to use the free tier.

Your documents (Free tier)

When you upload agreement files for comparison, those files are processed entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your documents are never uploaded to our servers, never transmitted over the internet, and never stored anywhere outside your device. The comparison engine runs locally on your computer.

AI analysis (Pro tier)

When you click a clause for AI analysis, the text of that specific clause is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. We send only the clause text you click — not your entire document. Anthropic does not use commercial API data to train its models. We are pursuing a Zero Data Retention arrangement with Anthropic for enterprise customers.

Session files (.maer)

When you save a session, the file is saved directly to your device. We do not have access to your saved session files.

Server logs

Our hosting provider (Railway) maintains standard server logs including IP addresses and request timestamps for security and debugging purposes. These logs are retained for 30 days.

No tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking technology.

Contact

Questions about data handling: info@contractscompare.com

Enterprise

Enterprise customers can request private deployment where no data leaves their own infrastructure. Contact us to discuss options.
