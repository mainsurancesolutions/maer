Security & Privacy

Your contracts stay yours

Confidentiality isn't a feature — it's the foundation. Here's exactly how your data is handled at every tier.

🔒 Free

Zero server processing. Your documents never leave your browser. All comparison and redlining happens entirely on your device using local processing — no files are uploaded to any server.

🛡️ Pro

Privacy by design. Anthropic does not train on commercial API data. Clause text is processed and returned — not stored for training purposes. Enterprise Zero Data Retention arrangements available upon request.

🏛️ Enterprise

Private deployment. Full on-premise or private cloud deployment within your firm's own infrastructure. Your documents never leave your network. Available with BAA and custom security review.
