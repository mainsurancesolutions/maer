A negotiation co-pilot that knows which side you're on

ContractsCompare AI analyzes your current draft against a curated library of real precedent agreements and suggests stronger language — based on your position. Whether you're negotiating a merger agreement, a real estate transaction, a derivatives contract, an insurance agreement, or any complex multi-party deal, ContractsCompare AI gives you an edge at every turn.

Buy-Side Mode

Identifies counterparty-favorable language and suggests buyer-protective alternatives drawn from negotiated precedent.

Sell-Side Mode

Flags overreaching positions and recommends seller-favorable language based on market standard negotiated agreements.

Precedent Library

Every suggestion is grounded in real negotiated agreements — not generic boilerplate. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.
